# Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

> [!IMPORTANT]

> Some information relates to a prerelease product that may be substantially modified before it's released. Microsoft makes no warranties, express or implied, with respect to the information provided here.


This article answers frequently asked questions (FAQs) about CloudKnox Permissions Management (CloudKnox).

## Is CloudKnox just for Azure?

Microsoft supports multiple IaaS, and helps customers manage access and reduce risk across Azure, AWS, Google, and other services.

## Does CloudKnox support Oracle?

CloudKnox currently supports Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud Platform (GCP), and Microsoft Azure.

## Does CloudKnox have Jenkins or Bitbucket integration?  

CloudKnox does not support Jenkins or Bitbucket integration.
